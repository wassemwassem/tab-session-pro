Tab Session Pro
Version 1.0.0 — Free & Open Source
Save your Chrome tab groups with their colors and names, then restore them in one click after a session expires. Perfect for NetSuite, Salesforce, CRM tools, or any workflow with many organized tabs.
Keyboard shortcut
Alt + S   Quick save current session
Permissions used
🗂️ tabGroups — read group names & colors
🔗 tabs — read tab URLs & titles
💾 storage — save sessions locally on your device
No data is ever sent to any server.
