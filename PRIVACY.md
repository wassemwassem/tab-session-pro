# Privacy Policy — Tab Session Pro

Last updated: March 2026

Tab Session Pro does not collect, transmit, or share any user data.

All data saved by this extension (tab URLs, titles, and group names) is stored 
locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never 
leaves your browser and is never sent to any server, third party, or developer.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or any remote 
services of any kind.
